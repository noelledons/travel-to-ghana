Why Ghana?

Rich History

Ghana was once the seat of a flourishing empire grown rich from the country’s massive gold deposits – the Ashanti kingdom.

Cultural Diversity

Music festivals and monthly traditional festivals are ways to loosen up and celebrate life, with music, dance and plenty of food.

Adventure

Ghana is home to spectacular national parks – Kakum, Bia and the Mole National Park, home to thousands of wildlife and exotic bird-life.
